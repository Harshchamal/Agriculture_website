Ecological Farming and Services

Services

Crop Protection

We market a wide array of crop protection products, and currently have a diverse range of low toxic, high efficacy crop protection solutions in the market to ensure low dosage for optimum results.

Seed and Planting Solutions

We market a selection of local and imported seeds and planting materials including paddy, maize, seed potato, and up-country and low country vegetable seeds.

Crop Nutrition

We provide specially designed fertiliser mixtures and specialty fertilisers to meet the needs of all types of crops grown in Sri Lanka, ranging from tea, coconut, and rubber to paddy, vegetables, spices, fruits and ornamental plants.

Agri Equipment

We offer a range of machinery for agriculture mechanisation, from tractors and rice transplanters to sprayers and combine harvesters, while marketing products comprising the paddy, tea, coconut and dairy sectors. We also provide protected agriculture products and related services from the construction of greenhouses to the installation of micro irrigation and landscape irrigation systems.

Animal Health

We market a range of animal health products from world-renowned research and development companies for pet care, livestock and poultry. We are committed to improving the health and productivity of animals through the provision of ethical and sustainable solutions.

Horticulture Exports

With over three decades of experience, Quality Seed Company (Pvt) Limited is the only hybrid flower seed production company in Sri Lanka. Hayleys Agro Biotech (Pvt) Limited utilises state-of-the-art technology within an innovative and scientific environment to provide horticultural, edible and flowering young plants through in-vitro tissue culture technology.

Hayvet Laboratory

The Hayvet Laboratory in Kuliyapitiya employs a highly qualified, professional veterinary staff who provides essential diagnostic testing services using state-of-the-art equipment to fulfill a long-felt industry need for a specialised diagnostic laboratory.

Overseas Business - Haychem Bangladesh Limited

Haychem Bangladesh Limited (HBL) is a 100% subsidiary of Hayleys Agriculture Holdings Ltd. registered in Bangladesh Joint stock companies in 1997. Haychem Bangladesh Limited has now become a prestigious Agro product and input provider among Bangladeshi farmers.

Pest Management

The Hayleys Pest Management Division adopts practical, effective, and affordable pest control solutions in keeping with the highest international standards.

Empowering the Lives of the Local Agricultural Community

The local agricultural community is what keeps us going strong. We believe in supporting our local farmers and creating a secure environment for them to grow and harvest their crops. Along with ethical business practices and sustainable solutions we are dedicated to preserving our agricultural heritage for the future.
